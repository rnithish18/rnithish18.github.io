About us

A small studio built to move fast

Kryzent Technologies is an MSME-registered software development company based in Namakkal, Tamil Nadu. We work with businesses and individuals who need real, working software — not just a proposal deck.

Our focus spans full web application development, AI-powered tools built on modern language models, and freelance engineering support for teams that need extra hands on a deadline. Alongside client work, we run structured internship and training programs, giving new developers real projects to learn from.

We're a young, technically hands-on team — small enough to stay close to every project, and serious about shipping software that actually works in production.
